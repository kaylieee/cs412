About Marie Curie

Marie Curie was a polish scientist born in 1867 who studied radioactivity. In her work she discovered two elements polonium and radium. Some of her accomplishments are being the first female nobel prize winner, the first to win two nobel prizes, and the only to win nobels in two scientific fields
